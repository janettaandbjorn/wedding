The story of our love

This is the story of how Janetta and Bjorn met in New Zealand:

The legends tell of a brave Nordic warrior who travelled far and wide in search of adventure and great fortune. On his journey he visited many a land and in time his reputation as a fierce underwater rugby player preceded him. Upon travelling to the Southern Hemisphere, the furtherest the Nordic warrior had ever been, he was to meet with his fate - some say 'his match'. For what lay before him that day as he set out to scale the fiercest mountain in the Northern part of the island of New Zealand, a land famous for its livestock and Maori tattoo artists… not to forget rugby players, was to change his destiny forever…

There upon the fringe of the rugged landscape of Mount Tongariro lay before him a treacherous journey. Mount Tongariro is a compound volcano in the Taupo Volcanic Zone of the North Island of New Zealand. For he was not afraid, in his company were a party of merry men and women all keen on following him to the ends of the land. One such merry women interested him greatly. She had long flowing locks, olive skin and a smile to warm even the hardest of hearts. The Nordic warrior at once set out to conquer the olive maidens heart. He knew this would be his greatest conquest yet!

These two brave souls that would eventually intertwine and make a family together, living happily ever after.
